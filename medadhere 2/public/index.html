How It Works

A simple workflow—with clinical-grade guardrails

1
Set your medication schedule
Add meds with dosage + daily reminder time. We track completion automatically.
2
Run safety checks
We map your drug names (RxNorm) and pull label text (openFDA) to detect potential interactions.
3
Get plain-language guidance
AI-generated insights translate safety signals into cautious next steps (not medical advice).
4
Stay consistent with insights
See adherence trends, your streak, and what’s most likely to be missed—then act.
Safety summaries are for informational purposes and may not include patient-specific factors. Always confirm with your pharmacist or clinician.
